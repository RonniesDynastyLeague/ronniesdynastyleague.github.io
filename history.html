Most teams had name changes establishing the league in cities all over the country and the league expanded. In the chaos of 2020, DKL founding member Walker Horne brought the Cleveland Hounddogs their first chip and an inaugural North Division Championship over the new expansion Erwin Elephants led by the infinite Anna Foropoulos out of the Southern Division.
